## Validation Criteria

- Left arrow navigates back one day (unlimited history)
- Right arrow navigates forward one day (max: tomorrow)
- Current date is displayed centered between arrows
- The dashboard and single list view update to show the selected day's data
- Past-day tasks can be toggled (catch-up entry)
- Tomorrow's tasks are visible (based on recurrence rules) but may not be toggleable

## Motivation

The primary interaction is "today" but life happens — the family goes out for the day and needs to enter results the next morning. Day navigation enables catch-up without breaking the daily frame. It's a Could because the app works without it (today-only mode), but it's important for real-world family use.
